KNOWLEDGE BASE

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our timepieces

Are your watches water-resistant?

Yes! All our timepieces are water-resistant up to 50 meters (5ATM). Our Diver collection is rated for 200 meters, perfect for swimming and snorkeling.

Do you offer international shipping?

Absolutely! We ship worldwide with express delivery options. Delivery times vary by location, typically 3-7 business days for international orders.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return your watch in its original condition for a full refund.

How do I care for my watch?

We recommend avoiding extreme temperatures, magnetic fields, and chemicals. Clean your watch regularly with a soft cloth. For mechanical watches, service every 3-5 years.

Does my watch come with a warranty?

Yes! Every MUBBY COLLECTION watch includes a 5-year international warranty covering manufacturing defects and movement issues.
